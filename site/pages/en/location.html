The Happy Nest is centrally situated at Schwamendingenstr. 77, 8050 Zürich and is easily accessible by public transportation or car.

Getting there by car

  • From inside the city: follow the signs for the motorway to Winterthur/Flughafen. Take the exit marked Schwamendingen (first exit in the Schöneich tunnel). After exiting the motorway, stay in the left lane and turn left towards Oerlikon/City at the first traffic lights. At the next set of lights, go straight (use the right lane). You are now on Schwamendingenstrasse, number 77 is about 200m from the traffic lights on your right.
  • From outside the city: use the motorway heading into the city, exiting at Schwamendingen (last exit before entering the Schöneich tunnel). Take the right lane and turn right at the traffic lights. You are now on Schwamendingenstrasse, number 77 is about 200m from the traffic lights on your right

Getting there by public transport

  • By bus: Approximately 20 meters from the "Zürich, Friedackerstrasse" stop on the number 75 bus.
  • By tram: Approximately 300 meters from the "Zürich, Salersteig" stop on the 10 and 14 tram lines.
