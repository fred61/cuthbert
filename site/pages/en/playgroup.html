At the Happy Nest we have made it our ultimate goal to provide a well equipped and interesting environment and to encourage your child's development and joy of learning, without undue stress or frustration.

The playgroup provides the children with a structured morning and begins with a short circle time. This will include songs depicting the letter of the week, the weather and seasons. Different objects, and materials from the shelves will be introduced and demonstrated which the child can then choose to use later.

Daily activities include:

  • Singing and dancing
  • Themed crafts in conjunction with projects e.g. seasons, animals, Christmas
  • Free creativity: painting, drawing, cutting and gluing. Working and experimenting with a variety of natural materials
  • Story time
  • Role play
  • Modelling with play dough, clay or papier-mâché
  • Snack time
  • Free play, including Montessori practical life and sensorial materials, puzzles, various building systems, train sets etc
  • Outdoor activities at the near by park
