General

We don't charge for school and public holidays or other occasions when The Happy Nest is closed (e.g teacher's illness). However, we do charge for days missed for reasons outside our responsibility, e.g. if you take your child on holiday during the school term.

Playgroup

The fee for attending The Happy Nest Playgroup is 46 CHF per session.

There is a one-off non-refundable fee of 150 CHF, payable upon registration.

A refundable deposit of four weeks fees is payable with the first monthly bill.

Fees are payable one month in advance, within 10 days of the receipt of our bill.

English Club

The fee for attending The Happy Nest English Club is 45 CHF per session. In addition, there is a charge for materials which can vary with the course requirements but is generally 25 CHF for the Summer and Autumn terms and 35 CHF for the Winter and Spring terms.

There is a one-off non-refundable fee of 50 CHF, payable upon registration. The registration fee is waived if the child has previously attended the Happy Nest Playgroup or Happy Music.

Fees are payable per term in advance, within 10 days of the receipt of our bill.

Happy Music

The Fee for Happy Music is CHF 35 per session.

There is a one-off non-refundable fee of 50 CHF, payable upon registration. The registration fee is waived if the child has previously attended the Happy Nest Playgroup or the Happy Nest English Club.

Fees are payable per term in advance, within 10 days of the receipt of our bill.
