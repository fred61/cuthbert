We have spaces available in the Beginners class of The Happy Nest English Club.

Course start and Lesson time

The course starts in the Autumn term, the first day of classes is Tuesday, October 23rd. Lessons are from 13:45 to 15:15.

Content

The course is based on the Jolly Phonics system. The class size is kept small (maximum of 10 children) to allow a maximum of personal attention to each child.

Children are taught the 42 letter sounds and motions and how to blend the sounds in order to read words. They learn the 42 Jolly Jingles songs and learn about Tricky Words. They are taught the letter names and how to read books by themselves.

Cost

The fee for attending The Happy Nest English Club is 45 CHF per session. In addition, there is a charge for materials which can vary with the course requirements but is generally 25 CHF for the Summer and Autumn terms and 35 CHF for the Winter and Spring terms.
